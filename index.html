Welcoming You to KVS RO Mumbai Unit

Smt. Priya Sen, Principal

It is my distinct privilege to welcome parents, students, and educators to the academic portal of KVS RO Mumbai. Our educational ethos centers on nurturing scientific curiosity, national pride, and human values in each student.

As a PM SHRI designated school region, we are equipping our classrooms with active Atal Tinkering Labs and digital smart boards. We strive to provide equal opportunity to boys and girls under NCERT/CBSE guidelines, creating leaders who will engineer a self-reliant India.

Smt. Priya Sen Principal / Education Officer
